Welcome to Skating School! we believe in the joy and freedom that skating brings to individuals of all ages. Nestled in Tirupathi, our school has been a cornerstone of the community since 2005. Whether you're a beginner lacing up your skates for the first time or a seasoned skater looking to refine your technique, our dedicated instructors are here to guide you every glide of the way. Our mission is simple: to cultivate a love for skating while fostering skills that extend far beyond the ice rink.
